Game Description

The objective of Zoo - Happy Animals is to complete zoo tasks that include shaving alpacas, showering elephant calves, leading acrobatic chimpanzees to banana piles, feeding watermelons to ravenous hippos and feeding alligators. What could go wrong with feeding alligators? If you don't complete the puzzle round with timing and precision, you won't get to the next round.
