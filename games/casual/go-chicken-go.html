Game Description

The objective of Go Chicken Go! is fairly simple. You must get a flock of chickens across two three-lane highways and a river with moving logs. Well, perhaps not so simple after all. An arrow will point at the chicken you will be tasked to help across the road. The arrow keys help move the chicken up, down, left, and right, and the space bar lets the chicken jump.

Controls

  • • Arrow keys: move the chicken
  • • Space bar: jump
