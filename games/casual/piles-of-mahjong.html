Game Description

Piles of Mahjong brings a fresh twist to the classic Mahjong formula with some exciting new dynamics. The game has most of the same foundational rules as a classic game of table mahjong, but instead of simply matching two tiles, your goal is to match three tiles from a pile featuring a mix of familiar patterns like circles and sticks, alongside fun new additions like bird and panda heads. To make a match, you must select tiles by clicking on them, and they’ll move to one of the seven empty spaces at the bottom of the screen.
