Game Description

Geometry Dash Meltdown is a rhythm-platformer game, which means your skill and precision mostly come from how good you are at keeping the pace of your clicks. The game's main character is a cube you control with your mouse. The cube automatically side-scrolls; you can only control its jumps to avoid spikes, saws, missiles, traps, pits, and plenty more cube-crushing challenges.
