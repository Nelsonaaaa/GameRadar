Game Description

In Sniper Shot: Bullet Time, an FPS game, you step into the shoes of a highly skilled sniper entrusted with the critical mission of taking down enemies from afar. As you enter this matrix-like grid, take a moment to familiarize yourself with your surroundings. Everything can easily look the same in this monochromatic background.
