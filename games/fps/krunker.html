Game Description

Join the hectic and fast-paced action of Krunker.io. Reminiscent of staple FPS games like Counter-Strike, reaction times and aiming proficiency are your greatest assets. Move quickly to evade fire and shoot sharply to take down your enemy with clean precision. You can jump into a game instantly for immediate action or choose custom game modes in the server menu.

Controls

  • • Use the WASD keys: move
  • • Left click: shoot
  • • R: reload
  • • C: aim / zoom
  • • Space bar: jump
  • • Shift: crouch
  • • F: spray paint
