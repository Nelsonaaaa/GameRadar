Game Description

Money Factory is a physics-based puzzle game where your goal is to amass a fortune by strategically multiplying your earnings. Money rains down from the sky—truly a dream come true—but most of it starts in small amounts, just single dollars. To grow your wealth, you'll need to make the most of the eight obstacles on your screen, each temporarily catching the falling money and boosting its value. Some obstacles add a fixed amount, like +1, while others multiply your earnings, such as x1.1 or x2. For example, if a dollar hits the +1 obstacle, you earn an extra dollar, but hitting x1.1 will increase your total by 10%.
