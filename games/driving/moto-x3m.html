Game Description

Sit back, hold on tight, and get ready for a thrilling bike ride (with pretty exciting music). Each level of Moto X3M ramps up the thrill with constant complex obstacles that will need precision, skill, and, most of all, patience. Don't get too distracted by the fun; every mistake comes at a price— and that price is time. And time is the key to nailing the coveted 3-star rating, where only the fastest, cleanest runs bring you victory.

Controls

  • • Press the up arrow key: accelerate
  • • Perform flips and other stunts: gain time
