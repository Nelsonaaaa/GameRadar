Game Description

Hit the road and weave through traffic in Traffic Rider, a high-speed motorcycle racing game where you're on the road for an endless road race. The faster you go, the bigger the rewards, so muster up some courage and overtake cars at speeds above 100 km/h to earn bonus points and extra time.
