Game Description

Super Star Car is all about speed, precision, and strategy as you race through an exciting career mode. Every time you finish a race in this mode, you’ll earn stars and money, helping you climb the leaderboard and unlock new opportunities. Use your winnings to upgrade your car strategically, boosting its performance and ensuring that every race feels fresh and thrilling.

Controls

  • • W or up arrow: accelerate
  • • AD or left and right arrow keys: steer
  • • C: change camera view
  • • Spacebar: respawn position
