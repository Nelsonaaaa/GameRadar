Game Description

Compete in a series of motorbike races and advance your career to new heights. Travel the land unlocking new circuits as you progress. Land first place to earn the most money! You’ll unlock new biking gear and money for completing races.

Controls

  • • WASD or arrow keys: drive
  • • Space: respawn
  • • C: change the camera view
