Game Description

The graphics and one-button gameplay mechanics of Drift Boss are reminiscent of 80s arcade games. But don't let this simplicity fool you. You will be drifting your car so much that you may find no time for blinking! To control the car, all you have to do is left-click to go right and release the button to go left. This means there is no easy way to go straight. It's a constant balancing act of drift mechanics.
