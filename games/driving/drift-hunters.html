Game Description

You start Drift Hunters by choosing your car and type of ride. You can upgrade the performance, including the brakes, weight, gearbox, turbo, and engine, with superior parts. You can also finish off your car in any color and enjoy a selection of rims on the house.

Controls

  • • Use the WASD or arrows keys: steer the car
  • • Use C: change the camera position
  • • Use left shift: shift up gears
  • • Use left Ctrl: shift down gears
